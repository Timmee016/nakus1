CEO Profile

Chief Executive Officer of Nakus Group of Companies is Mr. Newman, a dynamic and forward-thinking business leader known for his strategic vision and commitment to excellence. With a strong foundation in engineering and corporate governance, Mr. Newman has guided Nakus Group to become a trusted name across Nigeria’s Oil & Gas, Logistics, Catering, Marine, and Surveillance sectors.

Mr. Newman’s leadership has been defined by innovation, transparency, and people-focused development. He believes in building indigenous capacity and has created platforms for youth training and employment across various business units.

Under his watch, the company has implemented high-performing systems that ensure service delivery is fast, efficient, and aligned with international best practices. His dedication to quality, safety, and sustainable growth has attracted long-term clients and partners locally and internationally.

Mr. Newman’s influence extends beyond business. He is deeply involved in community development and education-focused initiatives, empowering underserved groups and promoting inclusive progress. His vision continues to shape Nakus Group into a beacon of hope and excellence in Nigeria’s business landscape.
