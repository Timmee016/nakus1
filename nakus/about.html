About Nakus Group of Companies

Nakus Group of Companies (NGC) is a reputable Nigerian conglomerate with diversified interests spanning Oil & Gas, Engineering, Surveillance, Transportation, and Catering. Headquartered in Warri, Delta State, NGC has established a commendable presence through consistent service delivery, professionalism, and innovation.

Established to meet the growing demands of Nigeria’s energy and infrastructure sectors, NGC comprises multiple subsidiaries, each specializing in core service areas while upholding a unified mission of excellence and integrity. We operate with a deep understanding of client needs and deliver tailor-made solutions through our competent workforce.

Our vision is to be the most trusted, diverse, and resilient indigenous conglomerate delivering premium quality services across Africa and beyond. To achieve this, we develop lasting partnerships with our clients by providing superior value through innovative and sustainable solutions delivered with integrity and excellence.

Our operations are guided by a set of strong core values: Integrity, Excellence, Accountability, Safety, and Sustainability. These principles form the foundation of our service delivery and internal culture.

NGC provides a broad range of services, including Oil & Gas procurement and logistics, civil and mechanical engineering, transportation for personnel and goods, professional catering for offshore and corporate settings, and surveillance/security solutions tailored for operational safety.

We operate through a decentralized system led by a Board of Directors and supported by departmental managers across business units. This structure encourages autonomy, performance-driven operations, and accountability while ensuring strategic alignment across all units.

NGC enforces strict Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) policies in compliance with both Nigerian and international standards. We are deeply committed to the safety of our workforce, the communities we serve, and the preservation of the environment.

Finally, as a proud indigenous company, NGC prioritizes the development of local talent and sourcing of materials in alignment with Nigeria’s Local Content Act. We believe in building a sustainable future for Nigeria through job creation, skills development, and community empowerment.
