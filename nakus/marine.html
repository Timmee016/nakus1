Marine Logistics

Nakus Marine provides efficient, safe, and compliant logistics services for the oil and gas industry, offshore construction projects, and coastal development initiatives. Our robust marine capabilities support complex operations with precision and reliability.

We operate a diverse fleet of marine vessels including supply boats, tugboats, crew boats, barges, and platform support vessels. Each is maintained to meet international safety standards and operational excellence.

Our services encompass offshore crew transfers, material and cargo transport, subsea equipment deployment, marine surveillance, and maritime consultancy. We also facilitate vessel chartering, mooring support, and 24/7 dispatch coordination.

With a commitment to environmental safety, risk mitigation, and client success, Nakus Marine remains a trusted partner for seamless marine operations in challenging environments.
