Surveillance Services

Nakus Surveillance offers top-tier security and monitoring solutions to safeguard businesses, assets, and personnel. We specialize in the design, deployment, and maintenance of advanced surveillance systems tailored to industrial, commercial, and residential environments.

Our services include installation of CCTV systems, access control, perimeter fencing with sensors, motion detectors, and remote surveillance monitoring. We also provide consultation on vulnerability assessment and threat mitigation strategies.

We leverage partnerships with leading technology providers to supply high-quality surveillance equipment — from night-vision cameras to intelligent video analytics platforms. Our team of licensed technicians and security experts ensures discreet, effective, and timely deployment of all systems.

Whether it’s securing construction sites, oilfield operations, marine terminals, or office complexes, Nakus Surveillance provides peace of mind through precision and proactive protection.
